# growth-marketing Operator Contract

Use this reference when output will become campaign copy, launch planning, funnel instrumentation, or public positioning.

Required contract:

- Audience and ICP.
- Offer, positioning, and proof boundary.
- Channel and funnel stage mapping.
- Campaign QA checklist.
- KPI definitions and measurement method.
- Post-launch review cadence and stop condition.

Do not write copy that claims field validation, enterprise proof, or commercial readiness without external evidence.
