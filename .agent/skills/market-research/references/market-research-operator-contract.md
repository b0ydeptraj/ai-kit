# market-research Operator Contract

Use this reference when research output will influence product, pricing, positioning, or build priority.

Required contract:

- Decision question in one sentence.
- Source list with freshness, authority, and direct relevance.
- Claim labels: verified, inferred, unknown.
- Competitor, ICP, pricing, and adoption signals separated.
- Decision impact for each finding.
- Open questions that would change the recommendation.

Do not present market guesses as verified facts.
