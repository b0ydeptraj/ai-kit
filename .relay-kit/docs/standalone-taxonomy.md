# standalone-taxonomy

Use this taxonomy to place existing or future layer-4 standalones without collapsing them into the hub layer.

## web-and-experience
Examples or likely members:
- agent-browser
- frontend or UI flows
- threejs
- ui-heavy implementation

## backend-and-data
Examples or likely members:
- backend-development
- databases
- API-heavy services
- jobs and queues

## platform-and-infra
Examples or likely members:
- devops
- deployment
- CI/CD
- observability

## commerce-and-integrations
Examples or likely members:
- shopify
- payments
- catalog or checkout flows

## media-and-content
Examples or likely members:
- media-tooling
- content pipelines
- asset-heavy workflows

Treat the taxonomy as a classification guide, not as a requirement to ship every standalone in one release.
